Purpose of the Study

The objective of this study is to determine whether the traffic on Wikipedia pages concerning disasters and emergencies can be utilized to gauge public awareness and interest.

Research Questions

  1. What factors contribute to the temporary spikes in traffic on disaster and emergency Wikipedia pages?
  2. What is the nature of the relationship between disaster and emergency Wikipedia pages in different languages?
  3. Is there a correlation between the traffic patterns of various disaster and emergency Wikipedia pages?
  4. Can external factors be leveraged to predict traffic on disaster and emergency Wikipedia pages?
  5. Is there a significant relationship between Google search trends and the view patterns on relevant Wikipedia pages during emergencies and disasters?
